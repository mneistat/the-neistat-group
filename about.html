About Matthew Neistat

People First.Always.

The Legacy

Real estate isn't what Matthew Neistat chose. It's what he was born into.

Growing up in Buffalo Grove, Matthew spent weekends walking job sites with his grandfather and uncle — both developers who built everything from single-family homes to high-rises across Chicago. He watched how they worked: the relationships they built, the care they took, the reputation they guarded above everything else.

His grandfather's rule was simple: "Always put people first." His father and uncle reinforced it constantly: "Emphasize reputation above all else." These weren't slogans. They were the operating system of a family that understood real estate as a human business.

His father went further. A CFO for a major developer, he walked away from that security to build houses himself. Matthew visited those homes as a kid — watching his father's leap of faith. That courage stayed with him.

The Broker

After earning a degree in hotel and restaurant management from Le Cordon Bleu, Matthew spent nearly a decade in hospitality — learning to read a room, anticipate needs, and make people feel genuinely cared for. When an opportunity to enter real estate emerged in 2012, everything clicked. Fourteen years later, he hasn't regretted it for a second.
