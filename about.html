Matthew Neistat Broker • Jameson Sotheby's International Realty

"Always put people first." Matthew Neistat comes from a family with deep real estate roots — continuing a legacy of developers and investors built on reputation and relationships.

Growing up in Buffalo Grove, IL, Matthew's father — a CFO turned home builder — inspired him to take his own leap of faith into real estate. After earning a degree in hospitality management and spending nearly a decade in the restaurant industry, Matthew made the leap and never looked back.

Now with over 14 years of experience, he brings the same dedication, attention to detail, and genuine care for people that defined his years in hospitality to every client relationship. Those values have helped him achieve recognition as a CAR Top 10% Producer.

Matthew handles diverse property types including single-family homes, condos, multi-unit properties, and land development — with notable achievements including leading the listing of a $10 million West Loop deconversion.

"I don't believe in burning bridges. The ripple effect of human connection is too valuable."

Featured in Chicago Agent Magazine and Chicago Real Producers, Matthew is proud to call Chicago home alongside his wife Jaclyn and their son Mason.
